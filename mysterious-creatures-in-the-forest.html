Mysterious Creatures in the Forest

Different from conventional books, each of these booklets is non-identical. Apart from different book covers, the 'creatures' inside the booklets have their own characteristics; they have certain colours, width and height but they all have different appearance in different booklet.

The following are examples of booklet covers. (Scroll from left to right.)

Examples of creatures.

The booklets were the outcome of a Python learning project. The outcome was generated in Drawbot. A completely different booklet will be generated each time.
